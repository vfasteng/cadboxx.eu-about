Solid Mechanical Models.>

All the models stored in our Sketchfab account are in GLB format. They are all designed by highly skilled CAD Professionals. They are Game-ready, and the single file includes textures/animations in addition to having full mechanical properties.

Model Assembly!

Assembly of the models takes place in the Assembler application which we have designed especially for the task. Easy to learn yet very effective, all 3D Models can be assembled together.

Let there be

Whether you need a single model, or a complex assembly of custom parts, cadboxx is for you. You can even have a model edited to meet your exact needs. Connect models from any 3D Software with live joints and constraints and then use it to develop an optimised assembly by adjusting variables live on-screen.
